About CanDreams Immigration

We make the visa process faster

CanDreams Immigration Private Limited specializes in Study Visa, Spouse Visa, Visitor Visa, IELTS & PTE preparation. Our vision is to offer our clients a variety of services in an efficient and transparent manner.

We constantly strive to provide our clients with the best solutions in a cost effective manner. We are a group consisting of Canadian study visa consultants who have extensive experience in study visa and higher education immigration.

We partner with top colleges, universities, and academic institutions to make international education accessible to everyone. We help students with every aspect of their application process including filing, program selection, university relocation, and post landing support.
