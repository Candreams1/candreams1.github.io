Study Visa for UK

Candidates who wish to pursue a short-term degree in the UK (such as English for Beginners or Vocational Courses) can come here as a student. This visa category corresponds to the standard United Kingdom (UK) Tourist Visa. This allows you to study for up to 6 months at a registered UK institution. International students wishing to study in the UK for more than six months should apply for a student visa instead. This type of student visa is evaluated using a points based system

Reasons why you should choose the UK as study destination are listed below
  • World-recognized degrees and certificates
  • Variety of courses to choose from
  • You can work while you study to support your daily expenses
  • Studying in the UK offers great value for money
  • A high rate of employability in UK itself after completion of study
  • Student satisfaction is high as It’s a welcoming and multi-cultural society

If you plan to enroll in a three-year or four-year undergraduate course, the basic requirement is that you have 60% in Year 12 and an IELTS score of 6. In some cases, depending on the courses you choose, a passing score of 5.5 is also required accepted. The four-year program includes 2 years of study, 1 year of internship, 1 year of study. For the master's program, you must have an undergraduate grade of 55% and an IELTS score of 5.5-6.5 depending on the course. To get a UK student visa, you need to show how you will finance costs such as tuition fees, daily expenses, accommodation etc. We encourage you to check out the extensive reviews below. Depending on your situation, we recommend that you arrange to show such a large amount of money on the visa application. Funding is an important criterion for a successful UK visa. If you intend to support your funding with a fixed deposit/savings account, both must be older than 28 days. You can show this amount in your parent's account or in your own name.

Some UK universities also have the option of enrolling in their courses without IELTS scores. If your IELTS test result is bad, please contact us. We have several options available. We will investigate your case in detail and do our best to get you a UK student visa.
